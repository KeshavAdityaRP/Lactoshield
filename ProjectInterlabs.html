Interlab

Our team SVCE_Chennai also participated in the Plate reader interlab study that iGEM conducted.Interlab is conducted to observe and compare the measurement methods throughout the world. It consists of two measurements in which we followed the Plate Reader Measurement.The interlab study is mainly based on the measurement of fluorescence data from three different GFP expressing constitutive promoter along with a positive and negative devices.

At, First the devices were empty but with the resupply of the devices from iGEM we could complete the interlab on time.The measurements were obtained from UV spectrophotometry and Fluorimeter and the results were interpreted and compared for the relative efficiency of expressing GFP in three different constitutive promotors.
